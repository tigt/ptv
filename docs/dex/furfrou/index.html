Furfrou is the Kalos icon, especially after Lysandre soured Pyroar’s popularity. In the wild, they use their sharp claws to cut their own fur. The results aren’t particularly stylish, so they jump at the chance for a groomer to beautify them.
Pokéfeature: When Furfrou gets a haircut, its Level 5 Ability and the move False Surrender changes based on its haircut. Haircuts can be given during Long Rests (or by a Stylist any time outside of combat).

  • Untrimmed: False Surrender, Tangling Hair
  • Heart: Heart Swap, Soul-Heart
  • Star: Water Shuriken, Victory Star
  • Diamond: Power Gem, Prism Armor
  • Pharaoh: Hex, Cursed Body
  • Kabuki: Role Play, Dancer
  • Matron: Teatime, Friend Guard
  • Dandy: After You, Rivalry
  • La Reine: Trop Kick, Queenly Majesty
  • Debutante: Follow Me, Enthrall
    (The GM and players are encouraged to collaborate to create more.)
