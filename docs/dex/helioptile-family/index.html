Some Heliolisk absorb so much sunlight when extending their collar that it actually hurts. During these times, Heliolisk glow bright yellow and fires off Thunders so powerful they sear through rubber. Heliolisk won’t leave the sun on its own, so it’s up to its Trainer to find shade.
