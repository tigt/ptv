Mime Jr. are popular acting alongside puppets in plays. A performer gestures backstage, and Mime Jr. mimics them. A particularly attentive Mime Jr. may even have dialog! Beware hecklers: Mime Jr. will mimic gestures right back at the audience if confronted.
