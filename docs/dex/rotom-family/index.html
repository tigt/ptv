Rotom’s electronic possession has been harnessed as a CPU for many modern inventions. They don’t mind helping Trainers out in exchange for the new form, but it can become a hassle when Rotom gets bored of the device and leaves it empty.
Pokéfeature: Rotom can change forms by possessing machines. Rotom’s forms each have a separate Movepool.
