Cottonee bunch up together for warmth in high cold winds, but also to offer each other better control over where they drift. It is one of the 10 species that do not get penalized for a ring out if it’s caused by a natural breeze.

Trainers who put Whimsicott through many battles can spend hours cleaning up all the debris stuck in its cotton. If Whimsicott gets too burdened by the mess, it will take off its cotton entirely and wait to regrow. This leaves it defenseless, so it would prefer to just be groomed.
