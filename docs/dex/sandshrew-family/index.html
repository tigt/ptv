Sandshrew

Sandshrew taking dust baths are famously adorable. They frolic in coarse, abrasive sand, smoothing their armor for faster rolling and slipping out of foes’ grip. Domestic Sandshrew seek out litterboxes or childrens’ sandboxes if not provided with sand to their liking.
