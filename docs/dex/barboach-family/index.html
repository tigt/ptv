Barboach’s whiskers are so sensitive they can feel differences in barometric pressure, making them a reliable way for sailors to predict the weather. If Barboach hides deep in its mud, the sun is going to shine strong. If they come up for air, expect a lot of precipitation.

Whiscash’s whiskers look soft and fluffy, but they’re very strong. If a Whiscash untwirls its whiskers, it can cause severe injury to a foe. The whiskers are full of nerves, so Whiscash prefers to use its earthquakes as a defense instead.
