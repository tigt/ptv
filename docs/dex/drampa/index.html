Drampa can walk on water or even fly after a running start, so it’s a classic Ride Pokémon on Alola. It’s not fast, but Drampa’s fur is soft and it always keeps riders safe. Even small children are allowed to ride Drampa unsupervised, something no other Pokémon can claim.
