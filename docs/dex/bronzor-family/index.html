Bronzong gains no benefit from the rain it brings — it seeks cover when the water falls. Ruin Maniacs Monthly theorizes that it learned to summon rain to bargain for tribute from other species. If Bronzong cannot find food, it buries itself and sleeps until somebody needs it again.
