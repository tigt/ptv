Froakie

Froakie can use their bubbles to make a variety of new sounds, croaking into bubbles of different sizes. It blows these bubbles away from its lakeside home to lure away predators. Trained properly, even a Trainer can pitch their voice with Froakie’s help.

Frogadier

When Frogadier blows a bubble around an object, it can stick that bubble to a wall and come back for it later. These bubbles can last for up to an hour, but pop quicker in the summer.
