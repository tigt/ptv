Hawlucha Man is the Masked Royal’s most popular opponent. They have clashed 200 times, but joined forces to beat Fezzie Gigantoman and his DynaMachamp. During Hawlucha Man’s betrayal by his Pokémon partner, it wore a different mask to showcase its evil intentions.
