Cranidos uproots trees it comes across with a single push, destroying any roots in the process. Shieldon and Cranidos are natural rivals because of this, and it’s believed the two locked each other into an arms race millions of years ago.
Alternate: Cranidos loves to headbutt reflective surfaces, mistaking its face as a challenger. Mirrors, hubcaps, photographs: even high-quality paintings are in danger when this Pokemon gets in a mood.
