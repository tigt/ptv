Aegislash has 2 Stances: Shield and Sword. Default is Shield.

  • Before Aegislash uses a damaging Move, switch to Sword Stance and swap its ATK/SATK with its DEF/SDEF.
  • Before Aegislash uses King’s Shield, switch to Shield Stance and swap its Stats back.
